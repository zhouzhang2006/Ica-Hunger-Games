# Ica-Hunger-Games
**Lil' Ica is hungry, help him consume all the delicious delicacies before the timer reaches zero!
**

Since the start of the _Amphoreus_ arc in the turn-based video game _Honkai: Star Rail_, the beloved little, bloated pegasus known to all as 'Little Ica' has captured the hearts of many fans (including myself). That is why I've decided to dedicate my very first project to creating a _Pac-Man_ inspired minigame revolving around Little Ica and his everyday cravings for food across the HSR worlds.

Goal: Similar to in _Pac-Man_, your goal is to manuever Little Ica around a 8x8 tiled 'maze' and collect as many delicacies from the many different worlds in the _Honkai: Star Rail_ universe (e.g. Belobog, Xianzhou Luofu, Penacony, etc). There will be a 2-minute timer than begins countdown the moment you move Little Ica and upon collection of a dish, you will gain 'points' that count towards your final score when the timer ends OR when you lose to the ferocious enemies in the maze (similar to the ghosts in _Pac-Man_). It is also worth noting that rarer delicacies will grant a bonus amount of points compared to normal, 'common' ones, so keep your eyes out for these special treats!
