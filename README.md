# Ica-Hunger-Games
Lil' Ica is hungry, help him consume all the delicious delicacies before the timer reaches zero!

Since the start of the Amphoreus arc in Honkai: Star Rail, the little, bloated pegasus known to all as 'Little Ica' has captured the hearts of many fans (including myself). That is why I've decided to dedicate my very first project to creating a Pac-man inspired minigame revolving around Little Ica and his everyday cravings for food across the HSR worlds.

Goal: Similar to in Pac-man, your goal is to manuever Little Ica around a 8x8 tiled map and collect as many delicacies from the many different worlds in the Honkai: Star Rail universe (e.g. Belobog, Xianzhou Luofu, Penacony, etc). There will be a 2-minute timer than begins countdown the moment you move Little Ica and upon collection of a dish, you will gain 'points' that count towards your final score when the timer ends OR when you lose to the ferocious enemies in the maze.
